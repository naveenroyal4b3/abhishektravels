Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 10, 2024

Abhishek Travels ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store and protect your information when you interact with us, including when you submit your details through Facebook/Instagram lead forms, our website, phone, or WhatsApp.

By providing your information, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location (city/state, if shared)

Travel-Related Information

  • Preferred travel dates
  • Package preferences
  • Number of travelers
  • Any additional information you choose to provide

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website or interact with our ads, we may collect:

  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Cookies & tracking data (Used for analytics and improving services)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for:

  • Responding to your travel enquiries
  • Providing Tirupati Darshan bookings
  • Hotel bookings, travel packages, and related services
  • Communicating through call, SMS, WhatsApp, or email
  • Sending booking updates, confirmations, and reminders
  • Improving our services and customer experience
  • Marketing and promotional communication (optional)

Important: We never sell your personal information to any third party.

3. Sharing Your Information

We only share your information with:

  • Travel partners, hotels, and tour operators (only if required to process your booking)
  • Service providers (SMS/WhatsApp gateways, analytics tools)
  • Legal authorities (only if required by law)

All partners receiving information are required to maintain confidentiality.

4. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted storage
  • Restricted internal access
  • Secure communication channels

However, no online transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing
  • Ask how your data is being used

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

6. Retention of Data

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to:

  • Complete your booking
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal requirements

Once data is no longer needed, we securely delete it.

7. Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies to:

  • Improve performance
  • Personalize user experience
  • Analyze website traffic

You can control cookie settings in your browser.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website or ads may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content.

9. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last Updated" date.
