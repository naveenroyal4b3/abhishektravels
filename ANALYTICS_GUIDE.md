### Traffic Metrics
- **Users**: Number of unique visitors

- **Bounce Rate**: Percentage of single-page visits
- **Average Session Duration**: Time spent on site

## Understanding Your Data

### Good Signs ✅
- Increasing traffic over time
- Low bounce rate (<60%)
- High average session duration (>2 minutes)
- Multiple page views per session
- Traffic from multiple sources

### Warning Signs ⚠️
- Sudden traffic drop
- Very high bounce rate (>80%)
- Very short session duration (<30 seconds)
- Traffic from single source only
